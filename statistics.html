Statistics

This data shows that many respondents are unsure of their stance regarding drug abuse in Singapore, with almost 75% of respondents having a neutral view towards abusers and 1 in 3 respondents think that there is no drug abuse issue in Singapore, implying that there is a need to teach people more regarding drug abuse so no one else would fall prey into drug abuse.

This data is worrying to see as it means that every 1 in 10 Singaporean youths have undergone conditions that tempted them to start drug usage, implying that there is a significant amount of motivation for drug usage among youths in Singapore. The most popular anti-drug effort chosen by respondents is to have more laws and policies.
